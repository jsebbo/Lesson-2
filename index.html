My favourite cities

Country City
Scotland Edinburgh
England Leeds
Ireland Dublin

Edinburgh

Edinburgh is the capital city of Scotland, and is a top tourist attraction. The best things to do in Edinburgh is shopping, getting some food and exploring the sights. The best thing however, is watching Heart of Midlothian FC at Tynecastle Park.

Glasgow

Glasgow is Edinburgh's little brother, also home to Hampden, the Scotland Football Team's national stadium. Glasgow is good for nights out and shopping also.

Dublin

Dublin, Ireland is the best place for one special thing: drinking. Ireland is the home of Guiness, so the Irish love to party and drink. It is a nice place to spend a couple of nights in to go out drinking, have nice meals and go shopping.

Leeds

Leeds is a great place, especially for Leeds Festival. Every year, people from all the UK gather down in leeds for one of the biggest festivals in the UK. With amazing music artists at each festival, leeds fest is a definite go-to each year.
